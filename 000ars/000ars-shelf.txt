

First 10 known Essential Mix episodes (1993–1994)
	1.	30 Oct 1993 – Pete Tong — first ever Essential Mix broadcast.  ￼
	2.	06 Nov 1993 – Paul Oakenfold — early trance/house set.  ￼
	3.	13 Nov 1993 – Andrew Weatherall — early UK dance icon.  ￼
	4.	20 Nov 1993 – Danny Rampling — one of the original UK club DJs.  ￼
	5.	27 Nov 1993 – Terry Farley & Pete Heller — UK house duo.  ￼
	6.	04 Dec 1993 – The Future Sound of London — electronic pioneers.  ￼
	7.	11 Dec 1993 – Pete Tong (second mix) — guest mix from the host DJ.  ￼
	8.	18 Dec 1993 – David Holmes — eclectic DJ/producer set.  ￼
	9.	25 Dec 1993 – Pete Tong & Dave Darrell — holiday special mix.  ￼
	10.	01 Jan 1994 – Snap! — New Year’s mix from the dance act.  ￼
